How Learnzy Works

1. Register or Login

Create a free account to get started. If you already have one, just log in using your email or username.

2. Explore Courses

Browse a variety of departments like Programming, Math, Business, and Design. Click “View Courses” to see more options.

3. Select & Pay

Click “Select” on the course you want. When ready, click “Pay Now” to confirm. Each course comes with a price label.

4. Track Your Learning

Use the “Profile” dropdown to view your info and selected courses. It updates automatically when you enroll.

5. Need Help?

Use the “Help” form to contact our team or send feedback. We’re always here to support you!
